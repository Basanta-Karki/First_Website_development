Watching Movies

Pirates of the Caribbean

The one of the Favourite movie is the pirates of the Caribbean. The pirates of the caribbean is a series of adventure fantasy films that follow the fearless adventure of Captian Jack Sparrow, played by Johnny Depp. The first movie was released in 2003 and was titled "The Curse of the Black Pearl". Since then, four more movies have been released, with the most recent being "Dead Men Tell No Tales" in 2017. The movies are known for their exciting action sequesnces, witty humor, and memorable character.

Avengers

The Avengers, series of super hero movies poduced by Marvel Studios is another most popular and the another Favourite movie. "The Avengers", was released in 2012 and brought together several Marvel superheroes , including Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, and the Hulk. Since then, more several move movies have been released, such as "Avengers: Age of Ultron" in 2015, "Avengers: Infinity War" in 2018, and "Avengers: Endgame" in 2019. The movies are known for their thrilling action sequence, exciting storylines, and iconic characters.
